Carrots and green beans sautéed recipe

What Carrots and green beans sautéed can do?

Learning tips by Dietary plan

Carrots and green beans sautéed can be a healthy option for people with Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) as it is a good source of protein, while being relatively low in phosphorus and potassium. Tofu is also low in saturated fat, and cholesterol-free. However, it is important to check the sodium content of the tofu, as some brands may contain high levels of sodium, which can be harmful for people with CKD. It is also important to limit processed foods, added sugars, and saturated fats, as these can be harmful to the kidneys. It is always best to consult with a healthcare provider or a registered dietitian for personalized dietary recommendations.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package extra firm tofu
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 dash hot sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon liquid smoke flavoring


    • How to Prepare Peppered Ground turkey

  • Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly spray a non-stick baking sheet with oil.
  • Slice tofu into 1/2-inch slices, and gently press excess water out of tofu.
  • Cut sliced tofu into 1/2-inch cubes.
  • In a bowl, stir together the soy sauce, maple syrup, ketchup, vinegar, and hot sauce.
  • Stir in sesame seeds, garlic powder, black pepper, and liquid smoke.
  • Gently stir tofu cubes into sauce. Cover, and marinate at least 5 minutes.
  • Place the tofu on the baking sheet in a single layer.
  • Bake in a preheated oven for 15 minutes.
  • Turn tofu, and bake until the tofu turns golden brown, about 15 minutes more.


    • Nutrition Facts

  • Calories ----------------- 168
  • Carbohydrates -------- 13g
  • Protein ------------------ 13g
  • Dietary Fiber ----------- 1g
  • Total Fat ---------------- 9g
  • Sodium ----------------- 1002mg
  • Potassium ------------- 246mg
  • Cholesterol ------------ 0mg
