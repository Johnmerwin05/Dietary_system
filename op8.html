Salmon salad recipe

What Salmon salad can do?

Learning tips by Dietary plan

Salmon salad can be a good option for people with Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) because it is a low-potassium and low-phosphorus source of protein that is also high in omega-3 fatty acids, which can help to lower inflammation in the body, lower blood pressure and improve overall cardiovascular health.

When preparing salmon salad, it's important to use low-sodium mayo or a homemade dressing made with olive oil and lemon juice. It's also important to avoid adding high-potassium ingredients such as avocado or tomato.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • romaine lettuce
  • cucumbers
  • bell peppers
  • red onion
  • Avocado
  • fresh parsley


    • How to Prepare Peppered Ground turkey

  • Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  • Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or silicone mat.
  • Place salmon on the baking sheet, skin-side down, season with salt and pepper and bake 10 to 12 minutes.
  • Remove from the oven and let it cool down.
  • Flake flesh and set aside.


    • How To Season Salmon For Salads

    The seasoning for your salmon really depends on how you plan on dressing your salad. Since we are using a light dressing, we have a lot of room to play around for the seasoning.
