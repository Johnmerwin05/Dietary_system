Food recommendation Water
DRINKS DISHES by Dietary plan

Water is essential for people with chronic kidney disease (CKD) as it helps to flush out toxins from the body and maintain hydration. Drinking enough water can help to prevent dehydration and kidney stones, which are common complications of CKD. Water can also help to reduce the risk of infections and blood clots, as well as improve the function of the heart and blood vessels.

Food recommendation Tea
DRINKS DISHES by Dietary plan

Herbal teas such as chamomile, mint, and green tea are good options for people with CKD. They are low in potassium and phosphorus, and have antioxidant properties.

Food recommendation Coffee
DRINKS DISHES by Dietary plan

Coffee is a good option for people with chronic kidney disease (CKD) as it may help to reduce inflammation and improve cardiovascular health. Drinking coffee in moderate amounts can also help to reduce the risk of type 2 diabetes, which is a common complication of CKD. Caffeine in coffee can help to improve cognitive function and mood, however it's important to limit the intake.

Food recommendation Milk
DRINKS DISHES by Dietary plan

Milk is a good source of calcium, which is important for maintaining strong bones, but it's important to limit the intake as it's high in phosphorus. Milk can also be a source of protein, which is important for maintaining muscle mass, but it's important to choose skim or low-fat milk to limit the intake of fat and calories.
